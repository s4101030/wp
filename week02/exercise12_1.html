Inception (2010)

Directed by Christopher Nolan, "Inception" is a sci-fi thriller that delves into the world of shared dreams. Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a skilled thief who specializes in the art of extraction, stealing secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. He is given a chance at redemption when offered a seemingly impossible task: to plant an idea in someone's mind, known as inception. The film explores themes of reality, memory, and the power of the mind.
