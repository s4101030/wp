The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Based on a novella by Stephen King and directed by Frank Darabont, "The Shawshank Redemption" follows the story of Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins), a banker who is wrongfully convicted of murder and sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over the years, he befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding (Morgan Freeman) and earns the respect of his fellow prisoners. The film is a poignant tale of hope, friendship, and perseverance in the face of adversity.
