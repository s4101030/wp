The Lion King (1994)

"The Lion King," an animated classic from Disney, tells the story of Simba, a young lion prince who is destined to become king of the Pride Lands. After the tragic death of his father, Mufasa, Simba is manipulated into fleeing by his wicked uncle Scar. With the help of new friends Timon and Pumbaa, Simba grows up and learns valuable life lessons. Eventually, he returns to reclaim his rightful place as king and restore balance to the Pride Lands. The film is celebrated for its memorable characters, captivating story, and iconic music.
